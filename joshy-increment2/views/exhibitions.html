Exhibit 1: Philosophy - What would you do?

An interactive exhibit showcasing the decisions made by the people around you. Upon entering the space, you are presented with a few different philosophical questions, and once you enter, you can view the responses from everyone as thought bubbles projected on the walls.

Exhibit 2: AI in the Physical World

This exhibit gives robotic bodies to various AI models. This allows visitors to interact with the newest AI in a physical space.
