Mission Statement

MonoMuse is dedicated to showcasing the intersecton of art and technology. The museum exhibits come together to create one, cohesive experience, inspiring visitors to learn more about arts and how technology can enable creative expression. The museum serves anyone who comes with an appreciation for the arts and an open mind.

To create a truly immersive experience, we focus on one topic or theme at a time. You can see the current theme on the home or exhibit pages.

Milestones

  • 2026: MonoMuse launched the MonoMuse Scholarship which allows students in the creative or technology field to pursue higher education and come back to showcase their work at MonoMuse.
  • 2020: MonoMuse moved to the current location (pictured above) to bring its exhibits to the public on a larger scale.
  • 2018: MonoMuse launched its first digital art summer camp for local middle and high school students.
  • 2016: The original MonoMuse museum was founded, just 10 minutes from the current location.

How to support us

Supporting Monomuse is a vital way to help preserve our collection and ensure that our educational programs remain accessible to the community. Because we do not currently process donations directly through our website, we invite you to reach out to our team via email to coordinate your contribution. Please contact us at donations@monomuse.com for information on how to send a check or to discuss other ways you can support our mission. Every gift makes a difference in our ability to maintain our exhibits and serve our visitors.
