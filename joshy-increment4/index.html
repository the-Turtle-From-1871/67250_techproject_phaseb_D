Welcome to MonoMuse

MonoMuse is a contemporary museum dedicated to exploring the intersection of art and technology. Through focusing on a single topic or person at a time, MonoMuse aims to immerse visitors in the history and stories of a person or place. Regardless of if you are a new or returning visitor, there is a new perspective to find in each exhibit.
